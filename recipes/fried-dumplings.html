Fried dumplings a la home

Description

This is ancient long time kept secret recipe for chinese dumplings

    Ingredients
  • Dumplings
  • Cooking oil
  • Favourite spices
  • Chili pepper
    Steps for cooking
  1. Heat up your pan
  2. Pour in a table spoon of cooking oil
  3. Sprinle your spices onto hot oil
  4. Add diced chili
  5. Add dumplings to pan
  6. Fry for 4 minutes
  7. Steam for 2 minutes
