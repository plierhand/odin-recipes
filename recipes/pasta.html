Pasta

Description

This will be the most mediocre pasta you will ever try, fast and easy, but nothing to boom your mind.

    Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Half/n/half
  • Basil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
    Steps for cooking
  1. Chop your onions
  2. Dice your garlic
  3. Heat up pan and throw in onions with a table spoon of oil
  4. Cook onions till they become transparrent
  5. Throw your slices of tomatoes and garlic into pan
  6. Cook and cook and cook
  7. Done
