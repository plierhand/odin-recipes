Rum Cocktail cubalibre

Description

This is a drink for people who have good taste and like a lot of limes in their life

    Ingredients
  • White or Dark rum
  • Lime
  • Cola
  • Ice
    Preparacion
  1. Put your ice in glass
  2. Pour in 50ml of rum into glass
  3. Drop one slice of lime into glass
  4. Top up glass with cola
